Free Zip / Unzip software and Rar file extractor.
Cross-platform file and archive manager.
Features volume spanning, compression, authenticated encryption.
Supports 7Z, 7-Zip sfx, ACE, ARJ, Brotli, BZ2, CAB, CHM, CPIO, DEB, GZ,
ISO, JAR, LHA/LZH, NSIS, OOo, PAQ/LPAQ, PEA, QUAD, RAR, RPM, split,
TAR, Z, ZIP, ZIPX, Zstandard.
