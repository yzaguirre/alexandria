Add
Copy your CA to dir /usr/local/share/ca-certificates/
	cp foo.crt /usr/local/share/ca-certificates/foo.crt
Update the CA store:
	update-ca-certificates

Remove your CA.
Update the CA store:
	update-ca-certificates --fresh
