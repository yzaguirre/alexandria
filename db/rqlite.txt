it gracefully handles leader elections,
and tolerates failures of machines, including the leader

Install
	wget https://github.com/rqlite/rqlite/releases/download/v6.0.0/rqlite-v6.0.0-linux-amd64.tar.gz
	tar xf rqlite-v6.0.0-linux-amd64.tar.gz
	cd rqlite-v6.0.0-linux-amd64
	./rqlited ~/node.1
MacOS
	wget https://github.com/rqlite/rqlite/releases/download/v6.0.0/rqlite-v6.0.0-darwin-amd64.tar.gz
	tar xf rqlite-v6.0.0-darwin-amd64.tar.gz
