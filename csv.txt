csvkit
https://csvkit.readthedocs.io/en/1.0.3/
	sudo -H pip3 install csvkit
Convert Excel to CSV:
	in2csv data.xls > data.csv
Convert JSON to CSV:
	in2csv data.json > data.csv
Print column names:
	csvcut -n data.csv
View csv files
	csvlook examples/testfixed_converted.csv
