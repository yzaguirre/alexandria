PcapPlusPlus is a multiplatform C++ library for capturing, parsing and crafting
of network packets.
It is designed to be efficient, powerful and easy to use.
It provides C++ wrappers for the most popular packet processing engines
such as libpcap, WinPcap, DPDK and PF_RING.
