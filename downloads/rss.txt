Canto operates from a configuration file, ~/.canto/conf.py. 
It creates a default configuration automatically when you don't have one, 
~/.canto/conf.py.example:
Refresh all feeds:
	$ canto -a
Update all feeds, and then run Canto:
	$ canto -u
List all feeds:
	$ canto -l
Quick help:
	$ canto -h
